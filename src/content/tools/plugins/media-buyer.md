The media-buyer plugin connects Claude to your ad accounts across Google Ads, Bing Ads, and Meta Ads. It runs practitioner-built workflows that turn raw account data into prioritized, dollar-quantified action plans. Read-only by design.

Built from managing 25+ ad accounts daily.
